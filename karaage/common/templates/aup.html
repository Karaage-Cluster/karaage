{{ org_name }} has an Acceptable Use Policy which relates to security and mis-use. All users of {{ org_name }}’s High Performance Computing Facilities must be familiar with, and abide by, this policy.

Security

  • I agree not to share my {{ org_name }} account in any manner.
  • I agree to use pass-phrases or passwords that are, to the best of my knowledge, secure and unknown to anyone else.
  • I agree not to attempt to subvert security measures; nor to attempt to access facilities, systems or data that I am not authorised to access.
  • I agree to advise the {{ org_name }} management if I become aware of any security breach or potential risk.

Usage

  • I agree to use the facilities provided in an efficient and economical manner.
  • I agree to use the facilities for only those purposes I have obtained approval.
  • If I use software covered by any license agreement on {{ org_name }} systems I agree to be bound by that agreement.
  • I agree not to use the {{ org_name }} facilities in a way that could be reasonably expected to offend other users.
  • I agree to hold the operators of {{ org_name }} blameless in the event that I suffer any loss as a result of my use of the {{ org_name }} facilities.
  • I accept that the facilities of {{ org_name }} may not always be available for my use in the manner that I expect and agree to hold the operators blameless in any situation where I am adversely affected by availability or any other aspect of the {{ org_name }} facilities.
  • I understand that I am bound by various state and federal legislation that, amongst other things, bans Discrimination, Harassment, Vilification, Bullying and Defamation and movement or retention of Illicit material.
